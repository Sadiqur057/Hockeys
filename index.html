Clients Question

We understand you might have questions before stepping onto the ice with Hockeys. Here are answers to some of our most frequently asked questions:

Our Equipment

We have latest and best equipment like skates, a stick, helmet, gloves, and shin pads. Our knowledgeable staff can help you choose the right gear for your skill level.

Hockey Training

We offer a variety of hockey training programs for all ages and skill levels, from beginner clinics to advanced skills development programs. Check out our program section for more details.

Private Lessons

We offer private hockey lessons with our experienced coaches. Private lessons are a great way to get personalized attention and focus on specific skills you want to improve.

Ski Touring

While we specialize in hockey, we can connect you with reputable partners who offer ski touring experiences in your area.

Booking

You can book ice time and training sessions online through our website or by calling us directly.

Pricings

Our pricing varies depending on the program, ice time, and duration. We offer competitive rates and packages to fit your needs.
